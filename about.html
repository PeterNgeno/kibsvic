About KIBSVIC LIMITED

Who We Are

KIBSVIC LIMITED is a Kenyan civil engineering and construction company focused on delivering reliable infrastructure solutions for both private and public clients. We specialize in non-housing projects, including bridges, culverts, weirs, tanks, gabions, and perimeter walls.

Our Mission

To provide long-lasting, cost-effective infrastructure that improves communities and supports development across Kenya.

Our Core Values

  • Integrity: We deliver what we promise.
  • Excellence: We aim for quality in all we do.
  • Transparency: We work openly with our clients.
  • Accountability: We take full responsibility for our work.

What We Do

Our key focus areas include:

  • Bridge & Mini-Bridge Construction
  • Box & Pipe Culverts
  • Gabion Installation
  • Weirs and Water Dams
  • Water Storage Tanks
  • Perimeter Walls
